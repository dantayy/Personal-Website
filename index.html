QuoteFault

Built a quote-submission service for the CSH weekly newsletter using Python packages such as Flask as well as a MySQL database. This was my first major personal coding project and it's currently being used by over 100 active members within the organization. It's still a work in progress, with multiple future improvements in the pipeline, as well as contributions from other members.

Sonagraphic

The idea for this game prototype came from the recurring thought of mine that my gaming music playlist should be more integrated into the games i was playing somehow. The hope is that by the end of the semester I'll be able to let the player grab music/audio from any video on YouTube (ala AudioShield) and then be dropped into an FPS arena with waves of enemies coming at them that take damage based on how in-tune the player is making their shots with the music being played (ala Harmonix's cancelled Chroma). Further down the line I'd like to stylize it to look cartoonishly gory (ala Kingsman). As of right now I'm learning C++ so I can code the algorithms I need to in Unreal 4 to get things running, as well as reseaching sound processing.
